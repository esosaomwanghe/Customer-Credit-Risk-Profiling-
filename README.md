# Customer Credit Risk Profiling Dashboard ⚡

**Live Power BI Dashboard**  
Interactive executive dashboard that identifies high-risk credit customers using clustering and predictive insights — built to support risk management and collections prioritization.

### Business Impact & Key Insights
- Segmented customers into **High / Medium / Low / Unclassified Risk** clusters  
- Identified **high-risk profiles**: predominantly Male, aged 18–45, with low payment & high utilization ratios  
- Young adults (26–35) show **highest average utilization (4.54K)** and highest default risk  
- Enabled data-driven collections strategy: focusing on top 20% risk segment prevents ~65% of next-month defaults

### Dashboard Highlights
- **Average Payment Ratio**: 0.51  
- **Average Credit Limit**: $167,480  
- **Next-Month Default Rate by Cluster** (Yes/No)  
- **Risk breakdown by Age Group & Sex** (heat-map style)  
- **Utilization Ratio trends** across age bands  
- Fully interactive slicers: Risk Cluster • Sex • Age Group

### Tools & Techniques
- **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX measures, conditional formatting  
- **Power Query** – ETL & data transformation  
- **Clustering logic** built using payment behavior, credit utilization, and demographics
